From Match Pass to Superfan

The per-match pass isn't an endpoint; it's the start of a journey to convert casual viewers into loyal, high-value annual subscribers.

Competitive Positioning

The "Regular Match" pass is priced competitively against FanCode, providing a low-risk entry point. Higher tiers are justified by superior, exclusive content like the IPL and India home games.

The Upsell Funnel

1

Acquire

2

3

Convert

The user recognizes the value and upgrades, becoming a long-term, profitable subscriber.
